COSMIC WONDERS

A Universe of Stories, Secrets, and Strange Light

Issue #1 — Mr. Wonder

The Cosmic Wonders universe begins with Mr. Wonder, the host and father‑brand avatar who guides readers through mysteries, timelines, and the hidden architecture of the cosmos. His presence anchors the anthology — a storyteller who knows more than he ever says aloud.

The McMinnville Encounter

A buried craft beneath a quiet Oregon vineyard. A wine that reverses age. A town that has forgotten what it once saw in the sky. Cosmic Wonders explores the legend, the truth, and the fractures between them.

The People

They walk among us — not as invaders, but as witnesses. Their story threads through timelines, disappearances, and the strange glow that returns every few decades. Cosmic Wonders reveals their origin piece by piece.

Timelines

Time is not a river. It is a vineyard — rows of possibility, each tended, pruned, or abandoned. The Timelines concept forms the backbone of future Cosmic Wonders stories, weaving together Mr. Wonder, The People, and the McMinnville craft into a single expanding canon.
